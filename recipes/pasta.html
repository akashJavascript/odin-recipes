Pasta Recipe

Description

A classic pasta recipe is a timeless delight. It starts with selecting the ideal pasta shape, cooking it to al dente perfection. A rich sauce, crafted with sautéed garlic, onions, tomatoes or cream, and a blend of herbs, is generously poured over the pasta. Grated Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs provide the finishing touch, creating a comforting and versatile dish that can feature sautéed vegetables, tender chicken, succulent shrimp, or savory meatballs. Whether it's a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion feast, a perfectly prepared pasta dish remains an irresistible and beloved choice on tables worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or farfalle)
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Ripe tomatoes or cream (for the sauce)
  • Herbs and spices (such as basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs
  • Optional ingredients for customization (e.g., sautéed vegetables, chicken, shrimp, or meatballs)

Steps

  1. Select the pasta of your choice (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or farfalle).
  2. Cook the pasta to al dente perfection in a pot of boiling salted water.
  3. In a separate pan, sauté minced garlic and diced onions until fragrant and translucent.
  4. Add ripe tomatoes or cream to create the base for your sauce, and season with herbs and spices like basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Simmer the sauce until it thickens and the flavors meld together.
  6. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the sauce, tossing to coat the pasta evenly.
  7. Serve your delicious pasta in bowls, garnishing with grated Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs.
  8. For extra flavor and variety, consider adding optional ingredients such as sautéed vegetables, chicken, shrimp, or meatballs.
